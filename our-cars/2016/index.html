Sunseeker 2016

After a long run with the 2010 car, we have finally begun the process of manufacturing our next solar car. This generation of Sunseeker will be lighter and more efficient than the previous car; you can see the pre-production specifications below.

Mechanical Specifications

Weight with Driver ~ 600 lbs (anticipated)
Body and Frame Carbon Fiber
Front Suspension Unequal A-Arm
Rear Suspension Unequal A-Arm
Layout Four Wheels (RWD)
Rims Aluminium (Custom)

Electrical Specifications

Solar Cells Sunpower C60 (391 total)
Solar Array Area 6 m2
Solar Array Output 1.34 kW
Battery Lithium Ion
Motors 2 x 6HP CSIRO with stationary axle
