--- layout: wrapped pageid: sponsors title: Become a Sponsor intro: We have below, our four sponsorship tiers. All sponsors are placed within a tier based upon the cumulative value of their donations. If you are ready to make a donation, you may do so via the secure WMU Foundation Giving Portal.

However, we are also in need of, and accept donations of materials, tools, and services. If you are interested in making a non-monetary donation, please send an email to sunseeker@wmich.edu so that we may coordinate. ---

Sponsorship Tiers

Platinum

Donation(s) of at least $10,000.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.1
  • Larger area reserved for logo on the car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website3, with the option to have a short description of your company.

Gold

Donation(s) ranging from $5,000, upto $9,999.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.1
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Silver

Donation(s) ranging from $1,000, upto $4,999.

  • Logo placed on the interior of the Solar Car.2
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Bronze

Donation(s) of, or less than $999.

  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Perk Details

  1. Logo on Exterior — Your logo is placed on the outer shell of the car. Size is based on the sponsor tier you qualify for.
  2. Logo on Interior — Interior placement will be located relative to donated part/material location. Logos may be visible while the array is removed. Array removal is common while on display or during maintenance.
  3. Logo on Website — Logo and company name will be found on the sponsor page
